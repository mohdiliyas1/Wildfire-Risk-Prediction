K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN)

We use KNN to classify fire risk based on nearest historical data points.

show more
  • Selected Features: DC, Temperature, Month_Dec, Month_Mar
  • Feature Engineering: One-hot encoding for months, scaling
  • Accuracy: ~62.5%
  • Use-case: Classifies new environments as 'Risk' or 'No Risk'

Why KNN for Fire Risk?

KNN is intuitive and effective for pattern recognition in wildfire data. It compares new data with past fire events to classify risk levels accurately.
