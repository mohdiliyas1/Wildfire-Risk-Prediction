🌲 What Are Wildfires?

Wildfires are rapidly spreading, uncontrolled fires that occur in natural landscapes such as forests, grasslands, or brush areas. These fires threaten ecosystems, wildlife, human settlements, and air quality, often resulting in devastating consequences.

⚠️ Causes & Key Indicators

Wildfires are commonly triggered by lightning strikes, prolonged dry spells, high temperatures, and human negligence. Critical indicators for wildfire risk include low humidity, dry vegetation, high wind speeds, and seasonal climate patterns.

🧠 Our Prediction Model

Leveraging machine learning, our wildfire risk prediction model uses selected environmental features to assess potential danger levels. The model aids early warning and supports decision-making for prevention and rapid response strategies.
